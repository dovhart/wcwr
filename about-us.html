About Us

Wild Coast Wilderness Resort is a beautiful hotel & fishing resort in Port Renfrew, BC. Friendly service, comfortable accommodations & amazing fishing are what we’re known for.
Book your next trip with Wild Coast Wilderness Resort and you will experience the world-class, friendly service that makes us famous.
Wild Coast Wilderness Resort & Charters invites you to explore the waters from Victoria to Port Renfrew, British Columbia.

EXPERIENCE WCWR
Fishing Vancouver Island is an experience you won’t forget on your day out with Wild Coast Wilderness Resort, you will be able to target trophy Chinook and Coho Salmon, Halibut, or Lingcod along the west coast of Vancouver Island. Enjoy prolific water, comfortable vessels and the abundance of marine wildlife, such as orca and humpback whales, eagles, marine birds and many other species. From one-day fishing charters to three-day fishing adventures, Wild Coast Wilderness Resort has the Vancouver Island fishing experience for you.
