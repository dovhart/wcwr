FAQs
Find answers to common questions about our packages and exclusions.
＋− WHAT IS EXCLUDED?
Our packages do not include fish processing, gratuity or fishing license. These services can be arranged separately if desired.
＋− CAN I ADD SERVICES?
Yes, you can customize your package by adding fish processing or gratuity. Please contact us for more information.
＋− WHAT IS INCLUDED?
Our Boat only includes the boat, guide, fuel, bait rods and tackle. 2 and 3 all-inclusive packages include everything in the 'boat only' plus food, and accommodation and swag. Fish processing and gratuity are excluded.
＋− ARE TIPS REQUIRED?
While gratuity is not inluded in our packages, it is customary to tip your fishing guide if you are satisfied with their service. The recommended tip for a good day on the water is $200/day and an ecellent day is anything more :) Our guides work very hard and appreciate your generosity!
＋− CAN I GET A REFUND?
Refunds are full refundable 30 days before your trip.
