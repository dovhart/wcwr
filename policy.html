POLICIES
PAYMENT
  • All prices are in Canadian dollars and are subject to applicable taxes.
  • We accept VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, Debit card and cash.
RESERVATIONS
  • Reservations are recommended.
  • We have 24 hour check in. Please inform us about late arrivals (after 6:00pm, PST)
  • Full amount is paid at check-in.
FACILITIES
  • All facilities are non-smoking and pets are not allowed. Extra charges for cleaning will apply to guests who ignore this policy.
  • We accept VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, Debit card and cash.
CANCELLATIONS
  • One night deposit is required for all accommodations, 7 day prior notice is required to cancel your accommodation reservation with out charge. Fishing charters have a 50% non-refundable deposit, if cancelled your deposit can be used towards a future reservation or be forfeited.
