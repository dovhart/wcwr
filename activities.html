Escape to the Wild Beauty of
Port Renfrew

Immerse yourself in breathtaking scenery and exclusive experiences at Port Renfrew Wilderness Resort. Discover the perfect blend of luxury and adventure, surrounded by wild rainforests and the stunning ocean beach.
Experience exceptional service with several different lodging styles to suit your needs. Whether you’re looking for an individual, couple, family, or group stay, Wild Coast Wilderness Resort has got you covered. Located in the heart of Port Renfrew, our resort allows you to easily take in the beauty that surrounds this stunning area. Our friendly team are here to ensure you have an amazing experience that you’ll never forget. Our Wild Coast Wilderness Resort Store is stocked with West Coast memorabilia, gifts, fishing tackle & local artwork.
