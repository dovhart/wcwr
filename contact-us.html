Contact Us

Port Renfrew is located on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, 2 hours west of Victoria on Highway 14. Located at the southern end of West Coast Trail. Driving from anywhere on the Island is a breeze, you can reach us via the pacific marine circle route by entering from Lake Cowichan or through Sooke. Our guests are always welcome to join us via float plane or helicopter, and don’t forget… you can always boat here 🙂

GETTING HERE
DRIVING FROM VICTORIA
  • Route on the Map
  • Take Highway #1 out of Victoria
  • Drive 5km then take the Sooke exit
  • Take Veterans Memorial Parkway for 4km and turn right on Highway #14 (Westcoast Road)
  • Continue on Highway #14 (25 minutes to Sooke, 1.5 hours to Port Renfrew)
