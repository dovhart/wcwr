Hiker huts

Whether you’re at the end of your hike or just heading out, there’s no better way to spend the night than in a comfortable, warm, newly built hiker hut. Our huts are close to the West Coast Trail, Juan de Fuca Marine Trail, restaurants and the West Coast Trail bus stop. Click below to book your stay, or find more details on the Hiker Huts, please visit the Hiker Huts website.
