Hiker huts
Whether you’re at the end of your hike or just heading out, there’s no better way to spend the night than in a comfortable, warm, newly built hiker hut. Our huts are close to the West Coast Trail, Juan de Fuca Marine Trail, restaurants and the West Coast Trail bus stop.
