Sitka Spruce Cottage
Our sitka spruce cottage is the perfect getaway for two with a private deck to take in the night stars and open forest views. Our studio provides privacy and charm with a natural wood beam stretching from the kitchenette to bedroom. This cozy space includes a full bathroom, basic kitchenette (no stove or oven), dining area, wifi, deck and a private propane fire pit.
