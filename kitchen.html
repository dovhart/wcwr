Coastal Kitchen Cafe
“The regulars might be known by name, but this is hardly a mom ‘n’ pop fare. This beachy, laid-back restaurant has been rated as one of the best vibes on Vancouver island. The focus is handmade, quality local ingredients and prompt friendly service. Aromas of ‘Drum Roaster’ coffee and freshly baked treats will keep you coming back for more. Our retail area offers apparel, books and keepsakes from local artists around the island! Now open year round, check out our current fall menu. Full breakfast menu until 11am, from then onwards our pizza oven fires up! We now have beer on draught featuring a range from Phillips brewing, sit back and relax in the sunshine on our beautiful patio, or cosey up inside to escape the rain.”
